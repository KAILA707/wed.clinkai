The CTU Danao Entrance and Exit Gate Policy: A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Entering the Campus

Students, faculty, staff, and visitors begin their journey by approaching the entrance gate. They must ensure they have valid identification for students, faculty, and staff, or sign in if they are visitors. This helps maintain security and proper entry tracking.

Step 2: Security Check and Vehicle Inspection

  • Security Check: All vehicles and personal belongings will undergo inspection by security personnel. This step is essential to ensure safety within the campus.
  • Following Traffic Rules: Vehicles must follow the designated lanes and parking rules. Unauthorized parking will result in penalties.

Step 3: Conduct Inside the Campus

  • Pedestrian Walkways: Pedestrians are required to use designated walkways to avoid accidents and ensure safe passage.
  • Prohibited Activities: Smoking, carrying illegal substances, or loitering near the gates is prohibited. Adherence to these rules is essential for a safe campus environment.

Step 4: Exiting the Campus

Once the visit or school day is complete, individuals exit the campus in an organized manner. It is important to follow the established paths to avoid congestion and ensure safety for everyone leaving the campus.

Maintaining a Secure and Organized Environment

This step-by-step process emphasizes the importance of security, order, and personal responsibility. By following these guidelines, everyone contributes to a secure and smooth-running campus environment.
